
## Module 1 — Introduction and Applications of AI

### Artificial Intelligence
- AI extends human capabilities to tackle tasks beyond normal human limits
- ML models are trained using supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning
- Three levels of AI:
  - Weak AI — specific domains only (Siri, Alexa)
  - Strong AI — diverse capabilities across unrelated tasks
  - Super AI — hypothetical, surpasses human intelligence in all domains

### Generative AI
- AI that creates new content — text, images, audio, video
- Powered by Large Language Models (LLMs) — neural networks that generate human-like text
- Key capabilities — content creation, human-like interaction, data augmentation
- Use case categories:
  - Domain specific — marketing, creative work, product development
  - Industry specific — healthcare, gaming, fashion, education

### Benefits of AI
- Automates routine tasks — virtual assistants, smart home devices
- Personalization — recommendation systems on streaming and e-commerce platforms
- Security — biometric authentication, fraud detection
- Enhances smart device experience

### Generative AI Use Cases
- Text — GPT, PaLM, Gemini → tools like ChatGPT, Google Gemini
- Images — Stable Diffusion, DALL-E
- Voice and Music — Murf, AIVA
- Video — Google Imagen Video, OpenAI Sora

### AI Chatbots and Smart Assistants
- Software programs that understand queries and perform tasks
- Evolution — rule based → AI powered → generative AI chatbots
- Benefits — 24/7 availability, multilingual, personalized, scalable
- Used in — customer service, e-commerce, healthcare, education

### AI Impact on Industries
- Manufacturing — AI robotics, image recognition
- Healthcare — medical imaging, predictive analytics
- Finance — customer service, investment analysis
- Retail — inventory management, cashier-less stores, marketing

### My Key Takeaway
Generative AI is not just chatbots — it covers text, image, audio,
and video generation. LLMs are the foundation of most modern AI tools.
